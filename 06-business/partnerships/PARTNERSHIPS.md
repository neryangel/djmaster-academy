## 6. Partnership Legal Framework

### 6.1 Standard Partnership Agreement (Affiliate)

```
Key Clauses:
1. Term: 1 year, auto-renew with 30-day notice
2. Commission: 10-20% per agreed tier
3. Exclusivity: None (both parties free to work with others)
4. Promotion: Both parties commit to 2-4 promotional mentions/month
5. Payment: Monthly payouts (net 30)
6. IP Rights: Both retain ownership of content
7. Termination: Either party can terminate with 30-day notice
8. Confidentiality: Standard NDA clause
9. Compliance: Partner must comply with our Terms of Service
10. Liability: Indemnification for each party's content/conduct
```

### 6.2 B2B Licensing Agreement (Site License)

```
Key Clauses:
1. Grant: License to use all course content for educational use
2. Duration: 1-3 years, renewable
3. Territory: Specific school/location only
4. Users: Unlimited students at licensed location
5. Payment: Annual or 3-year upfront
6. IP: DJMaster retains all ownership
7. Audit: DJMaster right to audit usage (once per year)
8. Support: Standard email support (24-48 hour response)
9. Termination: 30-day notice, no refund for early termination
10. Escalation: Limit 10 licensed instances per organization
```
